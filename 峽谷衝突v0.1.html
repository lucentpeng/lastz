Last War Z 峽谷衝突指示

版本：v0.1

戰鬥前準備

  • 請將所有部隊召回，並確保醫院無任何傷兵，才能順利進入戰場。
  • 峽谷戰不會死兵、不會被搶資源，請大家安心進行戰鬥。
  • 活動中心右側「峽谷爭奪戰」內有詳細規則與地圖，建議事前閱讀。
  • 遷城功能可無限使用（僅限時間內），請靈活運用，快速接近資源建築或安全區。
  • 若遭遇異常狀況，請將應用程式結束並重新啟動，通常可解決問題。

戰鬥指示

主堡等級 < 23：

  • 比賽開始時，立即佔領礦場、淨水廠與醫療所。
  • 若遭攻擊無法守住，請立即分享位置。
  • 軍事設施出現後也需盡快佔領，建築物可多人佔領以增加防禦力（優先於礦）。
  • 三隊皆可派出：主力隊守建築，其他隊採礦。

主堡等級 ≥ 23：

  • 優先目標是打下已被佔領的建築物（地圖右下角標紅）。
  • Justfun 負責搶奪能源核心，能源核心會於比賽開始後 20 分鐘與 30 分鐘出現。
  • 請 23 級以上成員協助掩護。
  • 若發現高等級（27級）對手，或自己無法應對時，請立即分享位置。

剩餘狀況請大家在戰場上靈活應變。

補充注意事項

  1. 今日指揮官為 Justfun 指揮官。
  2. 峽谷戰目標是佔領資源與建築物，佔領時間越久分數越高。
  3. 戰場右上角還有一隻巨型喪屍，擊倒可增加全隊攻擊力，牠會不定期出現。
  4. 比賽開始 20、30 分鐘會出現 能源核心，需搶下並帶回安全區。

Pre-Battle Preparation

  • Recall all troops and ensure no wounded soldiers in your hospital to enter the battlefield.
  • No troops will die or resources stolen in Canyon War — feel free to go all-in.
  • Check the “Canyon Clash” tab in the Event Center (right side) for rules and the map.
  • Teleport is unlimited during the event period — use it to move close to buildings or safe zones.
  • If you experience glitches, force close and reopen the app to fix the issue.

Battle Instructions

Main Base Level < 23:

  • At match start, immediately occupy mines, water plants, and medical stations.
  • If under attack and cannot defend, share your location right away.
  • Occupy military buildings when they appear. Multiple players can defend one building. Prioritize buildings over mines.
  • Deploy all 3 teams: Main team defends buildings, others mine resources.

Main Base Level ≥ 23:

  • Focus on capturing already-occupied buildings (marked red on the lower-right map).
  • Justfun will secure the Energy Core, appearing at 20 and 30 minutes.
  • Level 23+ teammates, please support and cover.
  • If you find strong enemies (Lv. 27) or need help, share your location for backup.

Other actions will need to be decided based on real-time battlefield conditions.

Additional Notes

  1. Justfun is today’s commander.
  2. Occupy buildings and resources — the longer you hold them, the more points you earn.
  3. A giant zombie appears at the top-right. Defeating it grants team-wide attack bonus. Appears randomly.
  4. Energy Cores appear at 20 and 30 mins. Capture them and return to the safe zone.
